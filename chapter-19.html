Chapter 19

Returning to Simplicity

Original Chinese

絕聖棄智，民利百倍；
絕仁棄義，民復孝慈；
絕巧棄利，盜賊無有。
此三者以為文，不足。
故令有所屬：
見素抱樸，少私寡欲。

English Translation

Throw away holiness and wisdom, and people will be a hundred times happier.
Throw away morality and justice, and people will do the right thing.
Throw away industry and profit, and there will be no thieves.
If these three are not enough, then look to the One.
See simplicity in the complicated.
Realize the plain.
Reduce selfishness.
Have few desires.
