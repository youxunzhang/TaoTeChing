Chapter 28

Returning to the Uncarved Block

Original Chinese

知其雄，守其雌，為天下谿。
為天下谿，常德不離，復歸於嬰兒。
知其白，守其黑，為天下式。
為天下式，常德不忒，復歸於無極。
知其榮，守其辱，為天下谷。
為天下谷，常德乃足，復歸於樸。
樸散則為器，聖人用之，則為官長，故大制不割。

English Translation

Know the masculine, but keep to the feminine:
receive the world in your arms.
If you receive the world, the Tao will never leave you
and you will be like a little child.
Know the white, but keep to the black:
be a pattern for the world.
If you are a pattern for the world, the Tao will be strong inside you
and there will be nothing you can't do.
Know the honorable, but keep to the humble:
be the valley of the world.
If you are the valley of the world, the Tao will be available to you
and you will return to the state of the uncarved block.
When the block is carved, it becomes useful.
When the Master uses it, he becomes the ruler.
Thus, "A great tailor cuts little."
