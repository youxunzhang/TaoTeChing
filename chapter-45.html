Chapter 45

Great Accomplishment

Original Chinese

大成若缺，其用不弊。
大盈若冲，其用不穷。
大直若屈，
大巧若拙，
大辩若讷。
静胜躁，寒胜热。
清静为天下正。

English Translation

Great accomplishment seems flawed,
yet it does not outlive its usefulness.
Great abundance seems empty,
yet it cannot be exhausted.
Great straightness seems twisted,
Great intelligence seems stupid,
Great eloquence seems awkward.
Movement overcomes cold;
stillness overcomes heat.
Stillness and tranquility set things in order in the universe.

Reflection

This chapter reveals the paradoxical nature of true greatness. What appears perfect may be flawed, while what seems imperfect may be perfect. True wisdom often appears as simplicity, and great accomplishments may seem incomplete. The chapter concludes with the power of stillness and tranquility to bring order to chaos, suggesting that inner peace is the foundation of universal harmony.
