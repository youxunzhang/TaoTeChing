Chapter 23

Acceptance

Original Chinese

希言自然。
故飄風不終朝，驟雨不終日。
孰為此者？天地。
天地尚不能久，而況於人乎？
故從事於道者，
道者同於道，德者同於德，失者同於失。
同於道者，道亦樂得之；
同於德者，德亦樂得之；
同於失者，失亦樂得之。
信不足焉，有不信焉。

English Translation

To speak little is natural.
A gale does not last all morning;
a cloudburst does not last all day.
What causes them? Heaven and Earth.
If Heaven and Earth cannot make things last long, how much less can man?
Therefore, he who follows the Tao is at one with the Tao.
He who is virtuous is at one with virtue.
He who is lost is at one with loss.
When you are one with the Tao, the Tao welcomes you.
When you are one with virtue, virtue is always there.
When you are one with loss, the lost are glad to see you.
He who does not trust enough will not be trusted.
