Chapter 68

The Virtue of Non-Contention

Original Chinese

善為士者，不武；
善戰者，不怒；
善勝敵者，不與；
善用人者，為之下。
是謂不爭之德，
是謂用人之力，
是謂配天古之極。

English Translation

A good warrior is not warlike;
A good fighter is not angry;
A good conqueror does not attack;
A good leader serves followers.
This is called the virtue of non-contention;
This is called using others' strength;
This is called matching Heaven,
the ultimate principle of old.

The Power of Non-Contention

This chapter illustrates the paradoxical nature of true strength and leadership. The greatest warriors win without fighting, the best leaders lead by serving. This is the virtue of non-contention (不爭之德) - achieving victory through harmony rather than conflict, aligning with the ancient principle of Heaven.
