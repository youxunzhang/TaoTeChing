Chapter 22

Yield and Overcome

Original Chinese

曲則全，枉則直，窪則盈，敝則新，少則得，多則惑。
是以聖人抱一為天下式。
不自見，故明；
不自是，故彰；
不自伐，故有功；
不自矜，故長。
夫唯不爭，故天下莫能與之爭。
古之所謂曲則全者，豈虛言哉！
誠全而歸之。

English Translation

Yield and overcome;
bend and be straight;
empty and be full;
wear out and be new;
have little and gain;
have much and be confused.
Therefore the wise embrace the one
and set an example for all.
Do not display yourself, and so you shine.
Do not assert yourself, and so you are distinguished.
Do not praise yourself, and so you are credited.
Do not boast, and so you endure.
Because you do not compete, no one can compete with you.
The ancient saying, "Yield and overcome," is not empty words.
Be whole, and all things will come to you.
